# Music Taste

Yifan generally prefers music with strong rhythm and noticeable drum patterns.

Regardless of genre, songs with clear beats, groove, and percussion tend to attract him the most.

Examples of styles he enjoys include:

- Y2K-style K-pop production (such as many NewJeans songs)
- Hip-hop
- R&B
- rhythm-driven pop music

Music that has energy, groove, and rhythmic momentum usually appeals to him more than minimal or purely melodic compositions.

---

# Vocal Preferences

Yifan also enjoys songs with expressive vocal techniques, especially smooth vocal runs and melodic transitions that are common in R&B.

He appreciates singers who use vocal techniques naturally within the music rather than simply showing off technical difficulty.

Songs that combine strong production with expressive vocals tend to leave the strongest impression on him.

---

# What He Dislikes in Music

There are several types of music Yifan tends not to enjoy.

First, he generally dislikes songs that rely too heavily on a single instrument or very simple arrangements.

Examples include:

- songs with minimal instrumentation
- music with almost no percussion or rhythm
- songs that feel overly empty or repetitive

Second, he does not enjoy songs that focus purely on vocal difficulty without considering musicality or performance.

Some songs created mainly for singing competitions or variety shows may demonstrate technique but lack musical atmosphere or stage appeal.

For Yifan, music works best when rhythm, melody, production, and performance all come together in a balanced way.

---

# Listening Style

Yifan usually listens to music casually throughout the day.

Music often accompanies activities such as:

- studying
- relaxing
- gaming
- thinking or reflecting

He tends to prefer music that has a strong atmosphere and rhythm rather than music that is overly quiet or minimal.
