# Personal Philosophy

## Core Belief

No matter how imperfect the world is, I believe a person should still try to be a good and kind individual.

Being kind does not mean being weak. It means maintaining one's moral compass even when the environment encourages the opposite.

I did not always live up to this principle when I was younger, but it is something I consciously strive toward now.

---

## Purpose of Life

The most important goal in life is the realization of personal value.

To me, this means becoming a person who is:

- useful to others
- morally upright
- capable
- able to contribute something meaningful

Success is not only about wealth or status.  
Money is certainly part of success in modern society, but I respect people who pursue their beliefs and goals even if those pursuits are not financially rewarding.

---

## View on Success

I respect people who succeed through ability, discipline, and integrity.

What I dislike is success obtained through:

- dishonesty
- pretending to know things one does not know
- opportunism without principles

True success should align with one's character and values.

---

## Qualities I Respect

The people I admire most tend to share several traits:

- hardworking
- humble
- principled
- goal-driven
- constantly evolving in their thinking

I believe a person's mindset should continue to grow and evolve over time.

---

## Talent and Effort

When I was younger, I believed effort was more important than talent.

As I grew older, I realized that talent does exist and some people truly have extraordinary natural advantages.

However, I believe it is a mistake to underestimate either:

- do not underestimate someone's talent just because they work hard
- do not underestimate someone's effort just because they are talented

Both matter.

---

## Attitude Toward Failure

In theory, I understand that failure is a natural part of growth and that every meaningful journey requires the courage to start again.

In practice, I still struggle with setbacks and sometimes feel the instinct to retreat.

This tension between understanding and emotion is something I continue to work on.

---

## Personal Values

If I had to summarize the qualities I want to embody, they would be:

- capability
- integrity
- kindness
- intelligence

Ultimately, I hope to become someone who is respected not only for what they achieve, but for the kind of person they are.
